  • How it works

  • 1. Choose a difficulty level

    2. Choose a main or sub-topic

    3. Click the "send it" button

    • You can choose a single difficulty level and corresponding topic or sub-topic, or you can mix and match!

    If you have a great question you'd like to add to the mix, click the "submit question" button in the navigation dropdown, and you'll be prompted to send me an email. When sending a question, please include these 4 pieces of information and I'll take care of the rest:

    1. Question

    2. Answer

    3. Difficulty level

    4. Subtopic

    • Found a bug, typo, or incorrect information? Click the "report problem" button in the navigation dropdown and you'll be prompted to send me an email. Please be as detailed as possible, and I'll take care of it ASAP!

    • Note: Some subtopics don't have any questions (like week 1 fed law). If you try to choose a combination difficulty/topic/subtopic and nothing appears, that's because we need questions for that specific combination, so please send some over if you have them!
